Do’s

•	 Think before posting; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate 
•	 Maintain a professional tone, especially on public platforms 
•	 Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation 
•	 Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings and limiting personal details 
•	 Use social media to build a positive digital presence (projects, achievements, skills) 



Don’ts

•	Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords) 
•	Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
•	Do not engage in online arguments or harmful discussions 
•	Avoid copying or reposting content without giving proper credit 
•	 Do not overshare or post impulsively without considering long-term impact
