Stay Safe Online – Checklist

General Safety Tips
•	 Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) 
•	 Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments in emails, SMS, or social media 
•	 Always verify the URL of websites before entering login details 
•	 Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software 
•	 Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details) with anyone online 
•	 Use only secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi

Financial / UPI Safety Tips
•	 Never share your UPI PIN or OTP, even if the caller claims to be from a bank 
•	 Double-check payment requests before approving; receiving money does NOT require a PIN 
•	 Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm 
•	 Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraudulent payments 

If You Are Targeted: How to Report
If you experience financial fraud, harassment, or a data breach, immediate reporting is the only way to freeze fraudulent transactions.
